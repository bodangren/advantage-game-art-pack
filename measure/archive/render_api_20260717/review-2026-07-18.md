## 4. Risks or blockers

- **Blanket 400 on all errors** (`render.ts` failures and any internal error in `pages/api/render.ts:42-44`): a wasm-init failure or rasterizer panic would surface as a client-fault 400 instead of 500. Low impact locally, misleading for future batch pipelines.
- **No retry after failed wasm init**: if `initWasm` rejects once, the rejected promise is cached in `wasmInit` forever — every subsequent PNG request fails until the process restarts.
- **Unrecognized formats fall through silently**: `format=jpeg` returns SVG with a 200 rather than a 400. The spec didn't define this, but it's a soft edge in the contract.
- Not a blocker for this track, but the working tree currently fails 4 fixture tests — that belongs to the active art-iteration track and needs re-freezing before its next commit.
